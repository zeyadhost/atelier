# Hazardous Atelier

A virtual bakery where you create dangerously delicious pastries from absurd ingredients and sell them to AI customers who may or may not survive.

## Features

- **Content Generation**: Ingredient-to-attribute mapping system
- **Market simulation**: AI-powered customers that review your concotion and rate it
- **Economic Layer**: Progressive mechanism for ingredient tiers (start off with normal ingredients, level up to radioactive ingredients, etc,.)
